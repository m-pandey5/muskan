About me

I am a researcher and developer with a focus on zero-knowledge proofs, STARKs and SNARKs, zkVMs and arithmetization, private bridges and intent-based cross-chain execution, NEAR and Solana systems, threshold cryptography and key management, proof verification and aggregation, high-throughput blockchain infrastructure, privacy-preserving protocols and accumulators.

I am currently in my final year of my Bachelors at I.I.T Delhi. I am the President of the Blockchain Society at I.I.T Delhi.

I have worked as a Cryptography Research Engineer at Encifher, and as a Backend Engineer at Umbra Privacy.
