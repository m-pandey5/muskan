I am currently in my final year of my Bachelors at I.I.T Delhi. I am the President of the Blockchain Society at I.I.T Delhi.

I have worked as a Cryptography Research Engineer at Encifher, and as a Backend Engineer at Umbra Privacy.

  • proofPositive (Zcomp) — ETH Mumbai 2024, main-track winner in Zero Knowledge — Modular CirC-inspired compiler pipeline with frontends for C, Rust, Python, Solidity, and ZoKrates. Each path parses source to an AST, lowers through CIRC-IR existentially quantified circuits (EQC), applies backend optimization, emits R1CS, and synthesizes Groth16 proofs with bellman (Spartan also supported). The flow is end to end from high-level code through intermediate constraints to verifiable proofs, instead of authoring circuits by hand.
  • Quark Layer — A decentralised proof verification and aggregation layer. Faster proof verification guarantees using cryptoeconomic security of restaked validators, called soft guarantee. A hard but slow proof verification guarantee using cryptographic security of zkVMs. Using aggregation to multi prove verifications and reduce on-chain verification costs. Reducing cost and latency by orders for proof verification(s).

Developed a custom CLI supporting peer discovery (ls p), chain inspection (ls c), and block creation with network-wide propagation (create b).
